This year's guests at CPH PIX

Every year we have the honor of welcoming international and Danish film makers, producers, actors, artists, and musicians to CPH PIX. 2018 was certainly no exception. See who visited the festival here.

Tuva Novotny wins main award at CPH PIX 2018

The New Talent Grand PIX went to Swedish director Tuva Novotny for her Norwegian debut feature ‘Blind Spot’, while Danish Rasmus Kloster Bro received a special mention for his first film ‘Cutterhead’.

Highlights from PIX 2018

This year's festival has truly been fantastic. New Danish talents, award-winning masterpieces by Alfonso Cuarón and Lars von Trier, mesmerizing film concerts and plenty of breathtaking film experiences.

PIX continues on Blockbuster

This year's festival is over but our collaboration with Blockbuster continues. That means that you can watch many of this year's films from your couch at home. Catch some of the titles you didn't get to see this year - or previous festival years.
